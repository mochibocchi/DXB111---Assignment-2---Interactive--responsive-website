What makes savoury foods in Japan so special?

Japan takes savoury foods very seriously as they're known for their meticulous preparation. Japanese cuisine places a strong emphasis on the quality and freshness of ingredients, resulting in dishes that burst with natural flavors. Come try out savoury foods in Japan!
